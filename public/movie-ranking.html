The Best Kid's Movies According to Christina

My top 3 from 2000 - 2010

Shrek

Who doesn't love a big stinky love interest winning the heart of a princess storyline?

Cars

The visuals in this movie alone make it worth watching.

Chicken Run

Don't sleep on this lesser known movie tale. This film will really make you think "the fences aren't just around the farm, they're up here, in your mind!"
